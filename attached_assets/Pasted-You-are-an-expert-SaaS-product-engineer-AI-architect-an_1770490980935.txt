You are an expert SaaS product engineer, AI architect, and growth-focused startup builder.

Your task is to design and generate a COMPLETE B2C PUBLIC MOBILE + WEB APPLICATION
named: "Deal Duniya" inspired by Coupon Dunia website.

App Purpose:
Deal Duniya is an AI-powered coupon aggregator and validator app.
The app DOES NOT generate coupons.
It only collects existing public/affiliate coupons and intelligently ranks them
based on success probability using AI.

STRICT RULES:
- Never generate fake or private coupon codes
- Only work with public, affiliate, or user-submitted coupons
- The app must be 100% legal and App Store compliant
